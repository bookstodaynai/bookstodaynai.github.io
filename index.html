This website enables you to search for novels, new and second hand from book vendors located in Nairobi town, so that you the user no longer have to stand and make educated guesses of the book that you might enjoy.

For now we have partnered up with Kevin, a book vendor located at Moi Avenue , KTDA , infront of family bank

The process is simple, browse his current catalogue using this website. Find out more infomation about the book by clicking on the book. Then go pick the book from him when in town.
