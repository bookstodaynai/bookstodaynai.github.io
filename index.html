This website enables you to search for second hand novels from book vendors located in town , so that you no longer have to stand and make educated guess of the book that you would like. For now we have partnered up with Kevin , a second hand book vendor located at Moi Avenue , KTDA , infront of family bank The process is simple , browse his current catalogue using this website, finding more infomation about the book by clicking on the book. Then go pick the book from him
